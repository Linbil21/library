General
What is the Library Portal?
The Library Portal is an online management system that allows students and staff to access library resources, borrow books, track fines, view new arrivals, and manage library records digitally.
How do I log in to the system?
Go to the Login page and enter your registered username and password. If you do not have an account, please contact your library administrator to create one for you.
What should I do if I forget my password?
Currently, password resets are handled by the library administrator. Please contact the library staff with your full name and student ID, and they will assist you in resetting your password.
Books & Borrowing
How do I borrow a book?
To borrow a book, visit the Digital Library section from the sidebar. Click on the ADD button next to the book you want to borrow. The system will record your name, the book title, issue date, and due date.
How long can I borrow a book?
Books may be borrowed for a period of 30 days from the issue date. After the due date, a fine may be applied. You may request a renewal if the book is not reserved by another borrower.
How do I see new book arrivals?
Click on New Arrivals in the sidebar. This section shows all recently added books with their titles and the date they were added to the library collection.
Library Fines
How much is the fine for overdue books?
The fine is ₱5.00 per day for each overdue book. Fines accumulate from the due date until the book is returned. You can view your current fines in the Library Fines section.
How do I pay my library fine?
Go to the Library Fines page and click the Pay button next to your record. Payment is processed through the library cashier. Once confirmed, the fine will be marked as Paid.
What if my fine is incorrect?
If you believe your fine record is incorrect, please visit the library office with your borrower's card and the book in question. A staff member will review and update your record accordingly.
Account & Profile
How do I log out of the system?
Click the Log Out button on the top-right of the header or at the bottom of the sidebar. This will securely end your session and redirect you to the login page.
How are new students registered in the system?
New students are registered by the library administrator through the Dashboard (Registered Students section). Students cannot self-register; they must be added by an authorized staff member.
